# Privacy Policy

## Data Collection
Targetym collects the following data to provide services:
- User account information (name, email)
- Organization details
- Goal, recruitment, and performance data you create
- Payment and billing information

## Data Usage
Your data is used to:
- Provide and improve our services
- Send transactional and marketing emails
- Analyze usage patterns (anonymized)
- Ensure compliance and security

## Data Protection
- Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest
- Access is restricted to authorized staff
- We use Supabase with Row-Level Security (RLS) for data isolation
- Regular security audits and monitoring are performed

## User Rights
You have the right to:
- Access your data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your account and data
- Data portability (export your data)
- Opt-out of marketing emails

## Contact
For privacy concerns, email: privacy@targetym.com

## Compliance
Targetym complies with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations.
